# Product Return

## Scope
Covers physically returning an item, whether for a refund, an exchange, or a
warranty replacement. The monetary outcome is governed by the refund policy;
this procedure covers the logistics of getting the item back.

## Return Window
Items may be returned within 30 days of delivery. They should be in resalable
condition with original packaging where practical. Defective or
incorrectly-shipped items can be returned at any reasonable time and never
incur a restocking deduction.

## Return Methods
1. Issue a prepaid return label for defective or wrong-item cases at no cost
   to the customer.
2. For change-of-mind returns, the customer may be responsible for return
   shipping unless a free-returns promotion applies.
3. Provide a return reference and instructions for drop-off or pickup.

## Exchanges
For size, color, or model swaps, create the replacement order on a hold and
release it once the carrier scans the returned item, or immediately for
trusted accounts. Explain any price difference.

## Inspection
On receipt, the warehouse inspects the item and confirms condition before the
refund or exchange is finalized, typically within 2 business days of delivery
back to the facility.

## Agent Steps
1. Verify the order and reason for return.
2. Select the correct return method and fee treatment.
3. Send the label and clear packing instructions.
4. Set expectations: inspection time plus refund timeline from the refund
   policy.

## Escalation
Disputed condition assessments or high-value items follow the escalation
matrix.
