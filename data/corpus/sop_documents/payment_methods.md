# Payment Methods

## Accepted Methods
The store accepts major credit and debit cards, common digital wallets, and
bank transfer for eligible regions. Gift cards and store credit can be
combined with one other payment method at checkout. Cash on delivery is not
offered for online orders.

## Authorization vs Capture
At checkout the payment is authorized (a temporary hold) and only captured
when the order ships. A visible pending hold that does not match the final
total is normal and resolves within 3–5 business days; explain this before
treating it as a billing dispute.

## Failed Or Declined Payments
Common causes: insufficient funds, an expired card, a billing-address
mismatch, or the bank's fraud screening. Advise the customer to verify card
details and contact their bank if the decline persists. Never ask the
customer to send full card numbers or security codes through chat or email.

## Updating Payment Details
Customers update saved payment methods in account settings. For a failed
subscription renewal, updating the method and retrying restores the
subscription without creating a new one.

## Currency And Fees
Charges are billed in the store's listed currency. Foreign-exchange
conversion or international fees are set by the customer's bank and are not
controlled or refundable by support.

## Agent Steps
1. Verify identity before discussing any payment record.
2. Identify whether the issue is an authorization hold, a decline, or a true
   charge problem.
3. For declines, guide a safe retry; for charge problems, follow billing
   disputes.
4. Escalate suspected fraud through the escalation matrix.
