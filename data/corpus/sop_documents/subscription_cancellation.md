# Subscription Cancellation

## Scope
Covers customers who want to cancel a recurring subscription, pause it, or
stop auto-renewal. Refunds for already-billed periods follow the refund
policy.

## Cancellation Options
- **Cancel at period end (default):** access continues until the paid period
  expires; no further charges occur.
- **Immediate cancellation:** access ends now; eligible unused time may be
  refunded only where the plan terms or local law require it.
- **Pause:** billing suspends for a chosen interval and resumes automatically
  unless cancelled.

## Auto-Renewal
Auto-renewal is on by default. Disabling it keeps the subscription active
through the current period and prevents the next charge. Confirm to the
customer the exact date the final access ends.

## Retention
Offering a pause or a downgrade is acceptable when the customer's concern is
price or temporary non-use. Do not apply pressure, and always honor a clear
cancellation request even if a retention offer was declined.

## Agent Steps
1. Verify identity and locate the active subscription.
2. Confirm which option the customer wants and the effective date.
3. Process the change and disable auto-renewal if requested.
4. Send written confirmation including the last day of access and whether any
   refund applies.
5. Log the cancellation reason.

## Communication
Acknowledge the request promptly, avoid dark patterns, and never silently
keep a subscription active after a cancellation request. Escalate billing
conflicts arising from cancellation via the escalation matrix.
