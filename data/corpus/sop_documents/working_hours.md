# Working Hours

## Support Availability
Live chat and phone support operate Monday to Friday, 08:00–20:00, and
weekends 10:00–18:00, in the customer's local business region. Email and
ticket support are accepted 24/7 and answered during staffed hours.

## Response Targets
- Live chat: under 2 minutes to first response during staffed hours.
- Phone: under 5 minutes average wait during staffed hours.
- Email or ticket: first reply within one business day; complex cases within
  three business days with interim updates.

## After-Hours Handling
Outside staffed hours, customers can self-serve through the help center,
order tracking, and the password reset flow. Critical issues — confirmed
fraud, account takeover, or a major outage — are monitored continuously and
actioned by the on-call queue regardless of the clock.

## Holidays And Peak Periods
On public holidays, support runs reduced weekend hours. During peak sale
periods, response targets may extend; set expectations honestly rather than
quoting standard times the team cannot meet.

## Time Zones
Always confirm the customer's time zone before promising a callback or
follow-up window, and express commitments in the customer's local time to
avoid missed connections.

## Agent Guidance
If a customer is contacting outside staffed hours with a non-critical issue,
acknowledge receipt, set a realistic next-reply time, and point them to
relevant self-service procedures. Do not promise immediate human follow-up
that the current staffing cannot deliver.
